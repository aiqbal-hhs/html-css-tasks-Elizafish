A brief explanation of the Sims franchise.

The sims is a simulation game designed by EA. It has four versions The Sims, Sims 2, Sims 3, and the Sims 4. There are three modes in the games, cas, and build buy, and live mode. CAS is create a sim where you can create people (known as sims) and design everything about them. Build buy is where you can build houses and decorate them. Live mode is where you can play out the lives of the sims in your household, you control them as a supreme overlord but they don't know you exist.
